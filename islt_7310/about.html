NINA BARNETT

“There is one word which may serve as a rule of practice for one’s life –"reciprocity"– Confucius.

I believe that the energy and effort you give to the universe and to the people around you will always come back to you. Therefore, I love to help people, give back, and I’m often told that I’m kind-hearted. I always try my best to remain true to myself, regardless of the negativity I am given by others.

My name is Nina Barnett, and I am from Chicago, IL. For the most part I’m an extrovert and going out to eat with friends or trying creative, spontaneous activities are my definition of fun. But you know what they say, “work hard, play hard”. I’m a hard-working person that loves to experiment and learn new things. I like to put my full effort into whatever I do, and I will work until I get the best possible result. Because of this, I’m well-rounded, and I have many different interests and hobbies. However, my top two passions are being a hair stylist, and pursuing graphic design. I have a hair business called “Shop Nina Imani”, where I provide many different services. Soon I will attend Paul Mitchel Hair School to expand my knowledge in the cosmetology field. Making people feel beautiful makes me feel great. Currently I am a student at the University of Missouri where I’m pursuing a Bachelor’s in Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies degree. Within this degree, I have component focuses on graphic design, business, and cultural studies. I am presumed to graduate in December 2022.
